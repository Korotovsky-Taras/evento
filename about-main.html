IT ALL STARTED WITH THE PERFECT CAST.
In 2017, Patrick Corley was fly fishing...

... near Aspen, Colorado when the idea to build a better corporate gifting company came up between casts. The following day, at Hunter S. Thompson's old haunt, The Woody Creek Tavern, that idea was solidified and Global Evento was born.

From our first office, a 25 ft Airstream Trailer (yes, we literally took the show on the road) to our recently renovated Central West End location, we couldn't be more proud of what we've accomplished so far.

Thank you to our clients, industry partners and Global Evento family for making that riverside idea a reality.
